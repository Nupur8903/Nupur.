OVERVIEW

Google Developers Student Clubs (DSC) is an initiative the company created to support a thriving global community of student developers. DSCs are university-based community organizations that give students a place to use Google technology while learning, collaborating, and creating.

GOALS

The Google Developers Student Clubs (DSC) program goal is information sharing, community building, skill development, and industry exposure with the goal of empowering student developers. DSCs enable students to use technology creatively, have a good influence on their communities, and connect worldwide through hands-on projects, creativity, and professional advancement opportunities. DSCs give students access to Google technology, connect academics and business, and foster a collaborative learning atmosphere that gets them ready for professions in the tech sector.
